The Ultimate Tab & Bookmark Manager – Organize, Save, and Restore Your Tabs

Too many tabs cluttering your browser? Toast is the ultimate bookmark manager for tabs and links, letting you save, organize, and restore them effortlessly. 

Whether you're working, researching, or collecting inspiration, Toast helps you manage browser sessions, save tabs, and organize your bookmarks without the clutter. Close tabs without losing them—Toast keeps everything you need accessible and organized across devices.

⚡ Powerful Features for Seamless Tab, Bookmark, and Session Management:
✅ Save & Bookmark Tabs – Save individual tabs or entire browser sessions with one click.
✅ Instant Restore – Open your saved tabs or entire sessions anytime, with just one click.
✅ Organize Bookmarks & Tabs – Group your saved pages and tabs by project, topic, or research.
✅ Cross-Browser Sync – Works with Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge, and Opera, syncing your tabs and bookmarks seamlessly. Available for iPhone.
✅ Cloud Backup – Your bookmarks and tabs are safely stored, ensuring they’re never lost.
✅ Real-Time Tab Sync – Instantly access saved tabs and bookmarks across all your devices.
✅ Easy Sharing – Share your organized tabs and bookmarks with a single link.
🔍 How Toast Tackles Tab Clutter & Boosts Productivity:
📌 Save & organize research – Easily bookmark pages from Google Docs, YouTube, Quora, and more.
📌 Declutter your browser – Keep tabs and bookmarks neatly organized into categorized collections.
📌 Effortlessly manage sessions – Save entire browser sessions, switching between them smoothly with no hassle.
📌 Focus on what matters – Close unneeded tabs without losing important ones, keeping your browser fast and organized.
📌 Sync across browsers and devices – Access all your saved tabs and bookmarks wherever you go.
🚀 Why Choose Toast as Your Session Manager and Bookmark Organizer?
➤ Organize your tabs and sessions – Toast helps you easily manage and switch between saved sessions, keeping your workflow smooth.

--- KEY FEATURES ---

Save Tabs Instantly
Save all open tabs in one click and access them anytime. Restore them later exactly as you left them.

Oversee Your Tabs
Cut through the chaos of scattered windows. Visualize and control all of your open tabs from a single, unified dashboard.

Rely on First-Class Protection
Recover tabs after browser crashes, restarts, or accidental closure with history that shows your windows and tabs exactly as they were at a specific moment in time.

Get Organized
Use collections to organize saved tabs by topic, timeline, or whatever suits your workflow.

Search Everywhere
Find what you need in seconds with unified search across open tabs, collections, and tab history.

Share With Flexibility
Move tabs and bookmarks freely in and out of Session Buddy with versatile import, export, copy and paste that includes support for 15+ formats including plain text, links, JSON, CSV, Markdown, and HTML.

Privacy-First Design
No accounts, no tracking, no data sharing. Everything stays local by default.

Solid As A Rock
Hoarding tabs at an industrial scale? No problem. Session Buddy is engineered for heavy workloads and can easily handle thousands of tabs and bookmarks.

--- COMMON USE CASES ---

- Save tabs before restarting your computer
- Switch to another session before a presentation
- Organize research
- Collect and share project links
- Reduce tab clutter
- Free up memory
- Restore tabs after a crash

Can Session Buddy recover lost tabs?
Yes. It automatically saves sessions and allows full restoration of windows and tabs exactly as they were right before the crash.

No. Data is stored locally for privacy. Opt-in cloud based storage will be offered in the future for convenience and additional data protection.

Yes. Access to all features is free and does not require an account. A cloud-based storage alternative, once offered, will be the only premium-level feature.
